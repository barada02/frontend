How to Play

  • Welcome to the Memory Card Game! The goal is to match all pairs of cards.
  • Each level has a different difficulty: Easy, Normal, and Expert.
  • Start with the Easy level. You must complete it to unlock the Normal level, and so on.
  • Click on a card to reveal it. Remember its position and find its matching pair.
  • If you successfully match all cards, you win the level!
  • You can track your progress and scores after each level.
  • After completing all levels, you'll see a congratulatory page with options to replay or exit.
